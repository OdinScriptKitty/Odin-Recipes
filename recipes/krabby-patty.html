Krabby Patty

Yes this is the one and only Krabby Patty. We have cracked the secret formula and it is horifying

Ingredients

  • 1 Seasame Seed Bun
  • 2 Mayonaise
  • 2 Pickles
  • 2 Slices of Cheese
  • 1/3 lb Crab Meat
  • 2 Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Take 1/3 lb of Crab meat and form into patties and season to taste
  2. Melt butter in a large nonstick frying pan or wok over medium heat. Add meat to pan and flip regularly
  3. Dress the bun with Mayo, Pickles, Tomatoes, and set aside.
  4. Flip the crab patties, place chees on top and cook to desired rarity .
  5. Finally, place patty oon bun and enjoy
